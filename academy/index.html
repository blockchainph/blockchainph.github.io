Certification Track

Blockchain and Cryptocurrency Specialist Certification

A structured certification pathway intended for participants who want a stronger foundation in blockchain systems, cryptocurrency concepts, and applied professional understanding.

  • Designed for practitioner readiness and structured learning
  • Suitable for professionals seeking a more formal certification track
  • Built for deeper progression in blockchain and cryptocurrency education

Professional Course

Blockchain Systems and Digital Asset Literacy for Professionals

A practical 16-hour non-technical program focused on blockchain systems, digital asset literacy, risk, regulation, fraud awareness, and professional judgment in real-world settings.

  • Built for professionals, managers, educators, and government personnel
  • Emphasizes literacy, restraint, and responsible decision-making
  • Supports readiness for compliance, risk, policy, and digital asset roles

Course

Introduction to Blockchain Technology

Foundational understanding of blockchain systems, core concepts, and real-world use beyond hype.

  • Clear entry point for learners new to blockchain
  • Focus on practical understanding rather than speculation
  • Suitable for professionals seeking strong conceptual grounding

Course

Introduction to Blockchain Investigations and Forensics

An entry-level look at tracing logic, investigative limitations, and the role of behavioral and on-chain analysis.

  • Introduces investigative thinking in blockchain environments
  • Explains what can and cannot be seen through on-chain analysis
  • Useful for risk, compliance, and investigative professionals

Course

Cryptocurrency Essentials

A practical primer on wallets, transactions, digital assets, common risks, and the basics professionals should know.

  • Provides a grounded understanding of digital asset basics
  • Covers risk, usage, and operational realities
  • Built for learners who need practical literacy, not trading hype
