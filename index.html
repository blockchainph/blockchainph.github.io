BPAP In Action

Recent engagements reflecting BPAP’s role in industry dialogue and capability-building.

March 10, 2026 • National policy and industry consultation

Ferdie James Nervida, founder and interim president of BPAP, represented the association in the DICT blockchain consultation as part of the wider industry dialogue on how blockchain can be advanced with stronger clarity, coordination, and long-term relevance in the Philippines.

Read the BitPinas feature

February 2 to 6, 2026 • DICT Region XI workforce capability-building

BPAP helped facilitate the Blockchain and Cryptocurrency Specialist Certification for the DICT Region 11 government workforce, supporting a stronger foundation for digital and blockchain capability in the public sector through practical training and active engagement.

University of the Philippines Diliman • Crypto at the Crossroads forum

BPAP was represented by Interim President Ferdie James Nervida in “Igniting Discussion: Crypto at the Crossroads,” a forum organized by UP SPARK and joined by students and professionals exploring the opportunities, risks, and financial relevance of cryptocurrency in the Philippine context.

About Us

BPAP exists to connect the people doing real blockchain work across the country.

We provide a professional platform for collaboration, knowledge exchange, and ecosystem development among practitioners helping shape blockchain in the Philippines.

Our Mission

To strengthen the foundations of a credible, responsible, and forward-looking industry.

BPAP supports the growth of blockchain in the Philippines through professional standards, meaningful collaboration, and practical initiatives that contribute to long-term public trust.

Why BPAP

The industry needs a serious, visible, and collaborative center of gravity.

Credibility

Help present blockchain as a disciplined and constructive field, not just a trend cycle.

Coordination

Create stronger links between practitioners, institutions, and stakeholders across sectors.

Adoption

Support practical use cases and informed engagement that can move the industry forward responsibly.
